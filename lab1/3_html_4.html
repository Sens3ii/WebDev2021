Scrambled eggs

  • Time: 6-7m
  • Level: easy
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 3-5
Butter
Salt and Pepper
Bacon

Steps: In a small nonstick over medium heat, melt butter (or heat oil). Crack egg into pan. Cover with a tight
fitting lid and cook 3 minutes, or until white is set. Remove from pan and season with salt and pepper.

Pizza

  • Time: 25-30m
  • Level: easy
Ingredients Quantity
Slim Lepeshka 1
Ham
Mushrooms
Cucumbers
Tomatos
Cheese
Ketchunez

Steps: Spread ketchunes on lepeshka. Chop all the ingredients, grate the cheese.
Lay out the ingredients beautifully on the pizza. Place in the oven for 10-15 minutes. Enjoy.
